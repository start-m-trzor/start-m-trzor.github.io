Starting Up Your Trezor Device

Welcome to Trezor.io/start, the official guide to setting up your Trezor hardware wallet. Follow these simple steps to get started with Trezor Suite (Version 22.9.3) and ensure your crypto assets are secure.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Visit trezor.io/start.
  2. Download and install Trezor Suite (Version 22.9.3).
  3. Connect your Trezor device to your computer.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your device.
  5. Securely write down your recovery seed.
  6. Start managing your crypto assets with Trezor Suite.

Trezor offers unmatched security for your cryptocurrencies, with a user-friendly interface and robust support. Whether you're new to crypto or a seasoned user, Trezor ensures your assets are safe.
